Our story

This kitchen was built on one obsession.

Before Arumai was a restaurant, it was a legacy kept alive in a small kitchen in Karaikudi. Rajkumar Pillai spent his childhood watching his mother dry red chilies in the sun and hand-pound masalas on a stone grinder.

Today, we preserve those exacting standards. No shortcuts. No commercial spice blends. Just the authentic taste of a Tamil home, served with the refined elegance it deserves.
